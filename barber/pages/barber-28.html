The Renaissance

Under Giovanni's leadership, Velvet Blade transformed from a neighborhood barber shop into a destination for discerning gentlemen. We introduced the first "appointment-only" model in the city, focusing on quality over quantity. The signature Royal Shave was born, combining Italian barbering techniques with Japanese precision.

Our chairs were imported from Milan, our tools handcrafted in Solingen, and our ethos solidified: every client leaves not just groomed, but transformed.
