Frequently Asked

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

Do I need an appointment?

While we welcome walk-ins, we highly recommend booking in advance to ensure you get your preferred time and barber. You can book online through our website or by calling us directly.

What products do you use?

We exclusively use premium grooming products from Baxter of California, Layrite, and other high-end brands that we trust for their quality and results. All products are available for purchase in our shop.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our clients at the rear entrance. Street parking is also available nearby with metered spots.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may be subject to a 50% service charge. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.
