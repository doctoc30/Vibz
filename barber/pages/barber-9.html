Frequently Asked Questions

What is the B18 AI Hairstyle Visualizer?

B18 is our proprietary AI system that analyzes your facial features and hair type to recommend and visualize the 10 most suitable hairstyles from our curated collection.

How do I book an appointment?

Use our online booking system above, call us directly, or visit our location. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for premium time slots.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, based on availability. However, we highly recommend booking ahead to guarantee your preferred time and barber.
