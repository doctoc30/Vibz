About Gentlemen's Edge

Where Tradition Meets Modern Grooming

Established in 2010, Gentlemen's Edge has redefined the barber shop experience by blending time-honored techniques with contemporary style. Our founders, master barbers with over 30 years of combined experience, envisioned a space where men could retreat, refresh, and revitalize.

We believe grooming is an essential ritual that builds confidence and reflects personal style. Every service at Gentlemen's Edge is performed with precision, care, and attention to detail that sets us apart.

Our shop features a warm, inviting atmosphere with premium products and tools. We're committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly products and practices whenever possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book an appointment?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially for weekends and evenings. For our senior barbers, booking a week ahead is advisable due to high demand.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a 50% charge of the service fee.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, appointments take priority, so we recommend calling ahead to check availability if you plan to walk in.

What safety measures do you have in place?

All our tools are sterilized between clients, workstations are sanitized after each service, and barbers wear masks upon request. We also have air purification systems installed throughout the shop.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer physical and digital gift cards in any amount. They can be purchased in-store or through our website and never expire.
